Introduction

3 Cornerstone Cottage is to be found in the heart of the village of Sedgeford, close to the local Public house with an outlook over undulating countryside. This historic rural village is recorded in the Domesday Book in 1086 . Sedgeford is set in an area of outstanding natural beauty, within easy reach of some of the most stunning beaches in the UK. It is within easy reach of beaches at Heacham, Hunstanton, Brancaster and Holkham Bay. The Cottage has one double bedroom, suitable for both couples or single guests. Very well equipped, clean and comfortable.
