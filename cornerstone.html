Introduction

This one bedroom, 14thC traditional chalk stone cottage, with it's brick kitchen extension has recently undergone a sympathetic, bespoke renovation throughout. It is a clean and cosy bolthole in the heart of the village and conveniently close to the King William IV, local country inn. The cottage is located in an area of outstanding natural beauty and just a short distance from some of the most stunning beaches in the UK on both the West and North Norfolk coastlines. There is a cosy log burning stove, large shared, south facing garden with wondrous views over the undulating hills and vales of rural Norfolk. The accomodation is ideal for single travellers and couples alike.
