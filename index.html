Introduction

6 Littleport Cottages is to be found away from the main road, in a peaceful location surrounded by open undulating countryside. It fronts Peddars Way, which joins the historic North Norfolk coastal path. Sedgeford itself is situated within a designated area of outstanding natural beauty, within easy reach of some of the most stunning beaches in the UK. Located just 2.5 miles inland, it is within easy reach of beaches at Heacham, Hunstanton, Brancaster and Holkham Bay. Within easy reach of Hunstanton, Burnham Market, Wells, Holt and Sandringham and many more besides. A diverse range of restaurants, hotels, pubs, shops attractions exist against the backdrop of a breathtaking coastline and unspoilt countryside. Whether you are looking for a beach holiday with the kids, dog friendly beaches and pubs/restaurants, walking or birding, discovering all the area has to offer, this cottage ideally located to do so.
