6 Littleport Cottages

Located on North Norfolk's Historic Peddars Way.

Introduction

This traditional stone cottage is sympathetically furnished throughout, a real home from home, clean, cosy and comfortable with a log burning stove. 6 Littleport Cottages is located in an area of outstanding natural beauty and just a short distance from some of the most stunning beaches in the UK on both the West and North Norfolk coastlines. Set away from the main road in a pretty Norfolk village on a private road, with a rural walk on the doorstep and enclosed large garden/sun trap that is ideal for couples, solo adventurers, small families and is Dog friendly.
