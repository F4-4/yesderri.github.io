Introduction

6 Littleport Cottages is in a designated area of outstanding natural beauty and just a short distance from some of the most stunning beaches in the UK on both the West and North Norfolk coastlines. Set away from the main road in a pretty Norfolk village on a private road, with a rural walk on the doorstep and enclosed large garden/sun trap that is ideal for couples, solo adventurers, small families and is Dog friendly.
