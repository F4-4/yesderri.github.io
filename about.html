About

The Area

Sedgeford is situated within a designated area of outstanding beauty, just a short distance from some of the most stunning beaches in the UK on both the West and North Norfolk coastlines.
The cottage is to be found away from the main road, in a peaceful location surrounded by open undulating countryside. It fronts Peddars Way which leads onto the historic North Norfolk coastal path walk. This traditional stone cottage has been sympathetically furnished throughout with comfortable furniture and beds which guarantee a good nights sleep. You will find the space spotlessly clean right down to the sparkling wine glasses! The kitchen is small but well equipped, the lack of a dishwasher means you must be able to wash and dry dishes. It is perfectly adequate for preparing pizza or a ready meal if you don't fancy dining out in the many bistro and gastro pubs or restaurants listed in the guide book. Watching the sun go down with a glass of beer or wine in the back garden is the way to spend a relaxing evening.
6 Littleport Cottages is a perfect way to enjoy a break without the owner breathing down your neck.
